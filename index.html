Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?

You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.

2. Is this website free to use?

Yes, the basic vocabulary and lessons are free for all users to help everyone learn English easily.

3. Do I need to create an account?

Creating an account helps you track your progress, but you can browse lessons without one.

4. How can I build my English vocabulary?

Practice daily using our 'Learn' section, listen to the pronunciations, and try to use the words in your daily conversations.

5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?

Currently, we focus on self-paced learning, but we plan to introduce certificates in the future.
